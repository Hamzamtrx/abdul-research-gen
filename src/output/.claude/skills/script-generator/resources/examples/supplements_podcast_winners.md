## UNIVERSAL PODCAST PATTERNS (All Scripts):

### 1. HOST DYNAMICS
- **Maximum 2 hosts** - Never more
- **70/30 split** - Dominant host tells story/explains, secondary asks questions
- **Natural roles**: Expert/Patient, Expert/Skeptic, Doctor/Client

### 2. INTERRUPTION TECHNIQUE
- Written interruptions create energy: "*Interrupts* But..."
- Shows urgency, creates realistic flow
- Usually 2-3 interruptions per script

### 3. OBJECTION FLOW
- Objections come from secondary host, not self-defense
- Each objection gets immediate address
- Common objections: alternatives (greens), practicality (where to get), mechanism (how does it work)

### 4. PRODUCT REVEAL TIMING
- Never in the first 30 seconds
- Usually after mechanism education
- Feels organic: "I take the ones by [brand]"

### 5. CONVERSATIONAL MARKERS
- "OMG", "frickin", "like", "yeah"
- Laughs written in: "*Laughs*"
- Casual language maintains relatability

### 6. NUTRITIONAL COMPARISON STRUCTURE
- Specific multipliers: "4.2x more", "100x more", "14,000x more"
- Always vs. common "healthy" food: spinach, kale, lettuce, broccoli
- 3-5 comparisons maximum per script

### 7. MECHANISM EDUCATION
- Root cause identification (deficiency, hormones, survival mode)
- Clear cause-effect chain
- Villain creation (often: "doctors won't tell you", "synthetic supplements")

### 8. AUTHORITY ESTABLISHMENT
- Personal long-term use: "I've taken it for 10 years"

- Medical credentials (when doctor format)

- Desperation moment: "I was having breakdowns every week"
